# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how this Discord bot and its related services (including the website and Discord OAuth2 login) collect, use, and store data.

## Data We Collect
The bot may collect and store the following data:
- Discord User ID
- Discord Server (Guild) ID
- Discord Channel ID (for configuration and logging purposes)
- Discord Role ID (for role management and verification)
- Moderation data (warnings, mutes, bans, kicks, and related logs)
- Verification status and verification-related metadata
- Economy data (virtual currency balance, transactions, and statistics)
- Leveling and experience data
- Invite tracking data
- Ticket system metadata
- User preferences and bot-related settings
- OAuth2-related identifiers used for authentication (no passwords are stored)

## Data Usage
Collected data is used solely to:
- Provide and maintain the bot’s features
- Enable moderation, security, and anti-raid protection
- Support verification and role assignment
- Operate economy, leveling, and engagement systems
- Provide optional web-based services using Discord OAuth2
- Prevent abuse and ensure system stability

## Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties.
Data may only be shared if required by law or to protect the service from abuse.

## Data Storage
Data is stored securely and only for as long as necessary to provide the bot’s services.
Access to stored data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

## Data Retention
Some data (such as moderation logs and economy statistics) may be retained for extended periods to ensure fairness, security, and abuse prevention.

## Data Removal
Users may request the removal of their personal data at any time by contacting the bot’s support channels.
Data will be removed within a reasonable timeframe unless retention is required for security or legal reasons.

## Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time.
Continued use of the bot after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

## Contact
For questions or data removal requests related to this Privacy Policy, users may contact the bot developer through the official support channels.
